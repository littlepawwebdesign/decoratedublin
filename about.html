About Decorate Dublin

Hi, I'm Andrea!

I was born in Verona, the city of love, where I learnt the craft of painting and decorating across the Scaligeri land and the Italian peninsula. During my work experience in Italy as a painter (2008-2015) I met a lot of craftsmen and women, great professionals that inspired my career progression, from the beginning as a young apprentice till I became a professional Master painter employed on hi-end residential and commercial projects.

For years I have been taught the foundations of painting and decorating and the old Italian craftsmanship passed through generations.

Later in the year 2015 I moved to Wellington, New Zealand where I worked on a wide range of projects within the Wellington city area. There I had the pleasure to work side by side with inspiring professionals and expanded my knowledge on the use of different paint finishes.

At the end of the year 2018 I moved to Dublin, Ireland where in early 2019 I started my painting and decorating business focused on domestic projects across County Dublin.

In the last few years I was awarded a National Certificate in Painting and Decorating from New Zealand and in July 2020 I was awarded the Associate Certificate in Interior Design with Distinction grade from Dublin Institute of Design.
